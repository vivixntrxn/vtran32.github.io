How the Game Works

Pickleball is simple to learn but has a few unique rules that shape how the game unfolds. Here's a quick overview of the court and the core concepts every beginner should know.

Key Concepts

  • Serving Rules

    Start each point by hitting the ball underhand into the oppoiste box.

  • Double Bounce

    After serving, the ball has to bounce once on your opponent's side and once on yours before anyone is allowed to hit it in the air.

  • Kitchen

    This is the area near the net. You can't step inside the kitchen to hit a ball unless it has already bounced.

  • Faults
    • The point ends if the ball goes out, hits the net, or fails the double bounce rule.
    • If a serve lands in the kitchen, it's considered out.
    • If the ball hits a player before it bounces, the play stops and the other team gets the point.
